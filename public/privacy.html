Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What We Collect

Almost nothing. LaserForge is designed to keep your data on your device.

Data Stored Locally (on your device only)

LaserForge uses your browser's localStorage and IndexedDB to store:

• Your projects and design files

• Application settings (machine setup, material presets, device profiles)

• Your license key or trial access code

• Camera calibration data

• Autosave data

This data never leaves your device. We cannot access, read, or recover it.

Data Sent to Third Parties

License verification: When you enter a license key, LaserForge sends the key to Gumroad's API (api.gumroad.com) to verify the purchase. Gumroad receives only your license key and returns a validation result. No design data, project files, or personal information is sent.

Payment: If you purchase LaserForge PRO, payment is processed entirely by Gumroad (gumroad.com). Gumroad collects your payment information and email address under their own privacy policy. We receive only your email address and license key from Gumroad.

Data We Do NOT Collect

• No analytics or tracking scripts

• No cookies (we use localStorage, not cookies)

• No telemetry or usage data

• No design files or G-code

• No IP address logging

• No advertising or third-party tracking

Your Rights

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. To delete all LaserForge data:

1. Open your browser's Developer Tools (F12)

2. Go to Application → Local Storage → delete entries starting with "laserforge_"

3. Go to Application → IndexedDB → delete the LaserForge database

Or simply clear your browser data for the laserforge.pages.dev domain.

GDPR / CCPA

Because LaserForge does not collect or store personal data on our servers, most data protection regulations do not apply to our software directly. Your Gumroad purchase is governed by Gumroad's privacy policy and data handling practices.

If you have questions about your data, contact us at support@laserforge.app.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
