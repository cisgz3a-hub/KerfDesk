Material-aware design

Tell LaserForge what you're cutting — plywood, acrylic, leather. Set the size. The software shows your material on the bed, exactly where it sits.

Designs auto-center on your material, not the bed. If anything extends past the edge, you see a red warning before you waste a single piece.

No other laser software does this

See before you burn

Toggle Preview mode and the canvas transforms into a realistic simulation. Dark burn marks on wood grain show exactly what your finished piece will look like.

Cuts appear as thin dark lines. Engraves show as darkened areas. Power intensity affects the depth. No surprises.
